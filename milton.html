Milton Real Estate

This rebrand was for a small, family owned real estate business. The rebrand included a new logo and color palette as well as stationary to accompany the brand. A new website was also created for the online presence of the company.

This logo and color palette was created with a modern audience in mind. The brand focuses on the green and gray to be the main colors. The orange is used a an accent in small ways throughout the brand and website.
