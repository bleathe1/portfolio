Women's Field Hockey Championship

This identity was created for the International Women's Field Hockey Chapionship in Berlin, Germany. This identity was meant to attract those interested in the event and advertise to others in the area. The identity consists of a large scale poster, a postcard, and an app for during the event.

This poster was created to be placed around the city to advertise the event to locals and tourists in the area.

This postcard was designed to be sent out to locals in the area and also fans of the teams that were playing in the tournament.

This app was designed to be used during the tournament, however could be used for fans that were unable to attend the tournament. It allows the user to see a scheudle of the 2 day event and which games are played on which day. It also allows the user to watch a play-by-play of the games, interact with the teams via social media and learn more about teams including the roster and team standings.
