Kent State Athletics

For the past year and a half, I have had the privilege of working for Kent State Athletics in the marketing department. Over the years, I have been able to create tshirts, social media posts, marketing materials, and more.

The Kent State Future Flashes is a club designed for young children to get involved with the athletics and attend sporting events for free. I was given the opportunity to redesign the website for the Future Flashes and make it more kid friendly and fun.

You can check out the site here: Kent State Future Flashes

Along with the website, I have created badges, postcards, a coloring book, and marketing graphics to promote the club.
