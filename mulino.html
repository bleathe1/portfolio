Mulino Cracker Packaging

This packaging was created for Mulino Crackers. Mulino is a high end grocery brand sold in stores such as Heinens and Whole Foods. The crackers came in 3 different flavors.

Inspiration was drawn from the colors and packaging of wine. The key elements being deep colors contrasted with simple light type and a clean design was inspired by many different modern designs. The sophistication of the marketplace inspired this concept.

For this packaging, my goal was to create a simple yet sophisticated package to appeal to a middle age crowd. The deep colors used provide a regal and sophisticated atmosphere to the packaging. Paired with the single weight type, a clean appearance is given. This brand makes its own statement by being placed in a higher end store, so the box design simply follows the higher end feel to take back home.
