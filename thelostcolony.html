The Lost Colony

This book was created to be a brief overview of the disapearance of the Roanoke Colony. The history of this topic can seem boring to some, so I wanted to make this book as simple as possible while still getting the point across. This book also gives the reader the opportunity to create their own theory as to what happened to the colonist after their disapearance.
