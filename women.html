Women in Science

This exhibit was worked on with a group of 2 other designers to be a preview of a larger exhibit at the Great Lakes Science Center.

This exhibit is meant to tell its viewers about some of the most influential women in science history. 5 women in 5 different fields are recognized as being some of the greatest minds in history.
