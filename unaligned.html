TypeCon 2018

This brand was created for the 2018 Type Convention. It consists of a convention booklet that holds a description of the event, a schedule for the week, and a breakdown of seminars being hosted. It also consists of a name badge given to attendees with a map and mini schedule inside.

This years conference will focus on the idea of alignment, more importantly, unalignment. As designers, we are taught early on that alignment and the grid are the basics and the backbone of modern design. Alignment creates a sharper and more organized layout. This year we are going against everything we are taught.

We are breaking the grid and sometimes completely ignoring it. We are creating tension between elements and making it uncomfortable. We are getting creative and throwing text all over the place. Getting back to the roots of our artistic expression is what we hope you can see throughout the event this year.
