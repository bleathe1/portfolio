Grow Your Own Crystals

This package was created for children and young teens interested in science and the creation of crystals. This kit includes everything you would need in order to grow a jar of crystals at home.

The brand was created to be delicate and colorful, influenced by the crystals being created. The colors and type used are meant to appeal to a younger audience.

This kit includes an instruction page, an infographic displaying different crystal shapes, salt packages used to grow the crystals as well as coloring liquid to make your crystals different colors.
