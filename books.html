Classic Book Rebrand

This rebrand was for 3 classic books. The goal was to give them a modern look to appeal to a younger crowd. From the 3 rebrands, 1 poster was created as well.

The books chosen were "The Crucible" by Arthur Miller, "Fahrenheit 451" by Ray Bradbury. and "The Odyssey" by homer.

The poster chosen to create was for the book "The Crucible". The poster represents the inverse of the art on the cover by contructing a shape out of the lettering on the inside.
