Hydrolyte

Hydrolyte is a rebrand of the current rehydration beverage Pedialyte. Pedialyte is currently seen as a drink for children and babies because it is often sold in that section of stores. My goal was to rebrand this beverage for all ages and lifestyles.

Hydrolyte is a rehydration beverage for all walks of life. Hydrolyte comes in 6 flavors (lemon, watermelon, peach, green apple, blue raspberry, and grape). This beverage has 2x the amount of electrolytes and 50% of the sugar compared to other popular rehydration beverages.

The current market is saturated with sports drinks meant for rehydration and full of electrolytes. Hydrolyte caters to a broad market from athletes, to babies, to adults and teens. They brand is colorful to promote well-being and the audience responds well to the color selection.

Hydrolyte is sold as single bottles in each flavor as well as in a 6-pack to allow buyers to experience all 6 flavors in 1 purchase.

This display will be set up in any grocery stores that already carry the Pedialyte brand. It will be used at the end of the aisle way that the product can be found in. This display is made of 100% recycled cardboard and can be broken down and folded for easy setup and transportation. The display will feature all of the single bottles with the flavors grouped together for easy identification.
